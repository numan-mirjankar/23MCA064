Did You Know?

The Sun is so big that approximately 1.3 million Earths could fit inside it.

More About The Sun

The Sun makes up 99.86% of the mass of the Solar System.

The Sun's surface temperature is about 10,000 Fahrenheit (5,500 Celsius).

The Sun's core temperature is about 27 million degrees Fahrenheit!

Every second, the Sun converts 600 million tons of hydrogen into helium.
